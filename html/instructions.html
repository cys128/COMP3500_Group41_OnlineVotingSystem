Event Introduction

The Hong Kong Metropolitan University voting event aims to involve all students and staff in campus decision-making through fair, just, and transparent methods. This vote will determine important campus matters for the academic year. All students and staff are welcome to participate!

Voting Rules

  • Voting Eligibility

    All current students and staff of Hong Kong Metropolitan University can participate in voting

  • Login Requirement

    Must register an account and log in before voting

  • Voting Limits

    Varies according to different voting items

  • Fairness Principle

    Prohibited activities like ballot stuffing. The system automatically detects abnormal voting

  • Result Validity

    Voting results will be announced after completion. Final results are based on system statistics

Voting Process

Step 1: Register Account

Visit registration page, complete necessary information to register

Step 2: Login System

Use registered username and password to login

Step 3: View Options

Browse information and descriptions of all voting options

Step 4: Cast Vote

Select preferred option and click vote button

Step 5: View Results

Check real-time voting results and ranking changes anytime

Important Notes

  • Please ensure to register with real and valid information
  • Read voting rules carefully before voting
  • Votes cannot be changed after submission, choose carefully
  • Contact technical support for system issues
  • Final interpretation belongs to Hong Kong Metropolitan University
